Adventure Notes
Walk around each Manila location, talk to people, then use the travel buttons to jump to the next place quickly. This version is about clue-chasing, not getting trapped in awkward navigation.
Move: WASD or arrow keys
Interact: E or Enter near a person
Travel: Q = previous location, R = next location
